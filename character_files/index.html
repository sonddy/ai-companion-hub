What Is This?

AI Companion Hub is a collection of interactive AI companions, each with unique personalities, voices, and 3D avatars. Chat with them using text or voice, watch them animate in real-time, and enjoy their personalized AI radio stations!

Each companion uses advanced AI (GPT-4) for intelligent conversations and realistic text-to-speech for natural voice responses. The 3D VRM models animate based on speech, creating an immersive experience.
